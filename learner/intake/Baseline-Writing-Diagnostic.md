# Baseline Writing Diagnostic Prompt

Complete this diagnostic in [learner/submissions/00-Baseline-Writing-Diagnostic-Response.md](../submissions/00-Baseline-Writing-Diagnostic-Response.md).

## Rules

- Do the first draft without AI assistance.
- Do not ask Codex, ChatGPT, or grammar tools to rewrite your text.
- A dictionary is allowed, but note if you used one.
- Keep your original wording even if you are unsure. The goal is assessment, not performance.

## Task 1: Personal Writing Sample

Write 150 to 220 words answering this prompt:

`Describe your current relationship with English. What can you do well, what is difficult, and what do you want to be able to write by the end of this learning process?`

## Task 2: Opinion And Argument

Write 250 to 350 words answering this prompt:

`Some people believe that AI writing tools make language learning easier. Others believe they slow real progress because learners become dependent on them. What is your view? Give reasons and examples.`

## Task 3: Formal Register

Write 140 to 220 words answering this prompt:

`You are writing to a course mentor. Explain your learning goal, the type of support you want, and the study routine you can realistically maintain. Use a clear and polite formal tone.`

## What I Will Evaluate

- clarity
- grammar control
- sentence variety
- vocabulary range and precision
- organization
- ability to match tone to task

## Submission Note

At the top of your response file, state:

- whether you used a dictionary
- how long the diagnostic took
- whether you revised the text before submitting
