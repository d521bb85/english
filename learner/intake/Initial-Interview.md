# Initial Interview Prompt

Answer these questions in [learner/submissions/00-Initial-Interview-Answers.md](../submissions/00-Initial-Interview-Answers.md).

Be honest and specific. Short answers are fine unless a longer answer is useful.

## Questions

1. What is your main reason for improving your English writing?
2. Why do you care about reaching a high level such as C1 or C2?
3. What do you currently do in English: work, study, reading, messaging, content creation, something else?
4. Which part of English feels strongest for you right now?
5. Which part feels weakest or most frustrating?
6. What usually happens when you try to write in English?
7. What kinds of writing matter most to you: emails, essays, messages, reports, notes, posts, stories, other?
8. How much time can you realistically spend per week on writing practice?
9. How often do you want short sessions versus longer sessions?
10. What topics are interesting enough that you would actually enjoy writing about them?
11. What topics do you strongly dislike?
12. What is your past experience with English learning?
13. Have you ever taken a formal English test? If yes, which one and what was the result?
14. What tools do you normally use while writing: dictionary, translator, grammar checker, AI, nothing?
15. What would success look like after 3 months of serious practice?

## Important Note

For the baseline assessment, it is useful if your interview answers reflect your real habits, not ideal habits.
