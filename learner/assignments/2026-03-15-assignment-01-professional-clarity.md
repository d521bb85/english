# Assignment 01: Professional Clarity

## Goal

Write two short pieces that are clear, natural, and complete.

## Why This Task

Your baseline shows that you already have enough ideas and useful vocabulary, but your writing loses quality when sentence control, phrasing, and completeness are unstable. This task targets those issues directly in formats that matter to you.

## Target Skills

- sentence control
- natural phrasing
- complete point development
- register control in practical writing

## Instructions

Write both parts in one file named:

`learner/submissions/2026-03-15-assignment-01-response.md`

### Part A: Work Message

Write a Slack or email-style message of `120-180 words`.

Scenario:

You need to tell a teammate that a task will take longer than expected. Explain why, what has already been done, what the main blocker is, and what you will do next.

Tone:

Professional, clear, calm.

### Part B: Short Opinion Paragraph

Write `140-200 words`.

Prompt:

`What makes technical writing clear and useful?`

Your paragraph should include:

- one main opinion
- at least two supporting points
- one concrete example

## Constraints

- Write without AI drafting.
- A dictionary is allowed.
- After the draft, do one self-review pass for up to 10 minutes.
- Under the response, add a short self-review:
  - what you changed during revision
  - which sentences still feel weak

## Success Criteria

- The text is easy to follow on the first read.
- Sentences are mostly correct and natural.
- Each piece feels complete, not cut short.
- The tone matches the task.

## Deliverable

One markdown file in `learner/submissions/`.
