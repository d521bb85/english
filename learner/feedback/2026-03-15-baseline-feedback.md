## What This Means

Right now, your writing looks stronger than basic B1 because you already operate in real work contexts and can express nontrivial ideas. But I do not yet have enough evidence to place you securely at B2, because B2 requires more stable control in extended responses, clearer paragraph development, and more natural phrasing under pressure.

## Recommended Focus For The Next 2 To 4 Weeks

1. Build cleaner sentence control in common patterns.
2. Improve natural phrasing in professional and practical writing.
3. Learn to develop one point fully instead of stopping after the core idea.
4. Start controlling register more deliberately in work-style writing.

## The Single Most Important Habit

Write short pieces regularly and always do a fast self-review pass focused on 3 things only:

- sentence correctness
- natural phrasing
- completeness of the point
